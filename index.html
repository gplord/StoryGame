Order

Choose whether the session will begin with Story→Game or Game→Story. The end of the first round will then flip the scenario and let you play the other!


What's the difference?

Story→Game focuses on first creating an interesting story scenario, and then shifting to thinking about how to "gamify" that scenario, creating rules and gameplay mechanics that serve the story well.

Game→Story flips this around, and first challenges players to think about what might make a fun game to play, and then shifts to trying to imagine how to create a story that fits that game idea and makes it more interesting.
